Page xxiii from sample chapter.pdf Richard Stallman used po (a portable peephole optimizer that performed optimizations generally done by high level optimizers in addition to those by low level peephole optimizers) as a starting point for the GCC optimizer. GCC and the sucessor to po, vpo, both still use RTL (register transfer list/language) as their intermediate language/form. Nathan Moore (moore_mn@cobra.cs.mercer.edu)
